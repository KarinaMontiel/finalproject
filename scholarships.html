Scholarships

HBCU Scholars Program: Thurgood Marshall College Fund

Students will receive up to $25,000. Any person of color can apply to this scholarship.

Due Date: Mid July

Hispanic College Fund:

The Hispanic College Fund and google offer $10,000 scholarships. The applicant must be of Hispanic Heritage.

Due Date: Early April

Ph.D. Engineering Fellowship:

The GEM Ph.D. Engineering Fellowship is available to minority students who are pursuing a Ph.D. degree in engineering. You must have an M.S. degree or currently be enrolled in an M.S. engineering degree program with a minimum 3.0 GPA to be considered for this award.

Society of High School Scholars Foundation:

The NSHSS provides STEM scholarships for underrepresented groups in order to help reduce financial barriersfor those with academic and leadership potential and the desire to pursue STEM careers.The Foundation awards this scholarship to high school seniors with a minimum GPA of 3.0 who are pursuing a degree in, or closely related to, one of the following STEM disciplines:

  • Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering
  • Biosciences/Pre-med/health-related fields
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Physics
  • Operations Research
  • Oceanography
  • Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Mathematics
  • Materials Science and Engineering
  • Information Sciences
  • Geosciences
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer and Computational Sciences, including Statistics and Accounting
  • Cognitive, Neural, and Behavioral Sciences
  • Civil Engineering
  • Chemistry

DUE DATE: EARLY APRIL
